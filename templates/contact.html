Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GrowFarm

GrowFarm connects farmers seeking funding with investors looking for agricultural opportunities. Farmers create profiles and list their projects with details about funding needs, expected returns, and timelines. Investors browse projects, invest in ones they believe in, and track their investments through our platform.

To invest in a project, first create an investor account and complete your profile. Browse available projects, view their details, and when you find one you're interested in, click the "Invest Now" button. Enter the amount you want to invest (minimum $100), and confirm your investment.

Create a farmer account, complete your farm profile with details about your operation, and then click on "Create Project" in your dashboard. Fill out the project details including funding goal, expected ROI, duration, and risk level. Once submitted, your project will be reviewed and, if approved, listed on our platform for potential investors.

We accept a wide range of agricultural projects including crop production, livestock, dairy, organic farming, sustainable agriculture, agroforestry, and more. Projects should have clear objectives, realistic timelines, and transparent financial projections. We particularly encourage sustainable and regenerative farming practices.

Returns are distributed according to the agreement specified in each project. Typically, farmers will provide regular updates on the project's progress and make payments to investors based on the project's timeline and success. The expected ROI and payment schedule are clearly outlined in the project details.
